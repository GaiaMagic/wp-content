
TwitThis for WordPress
v.1.0
-------------------------------------------------

INSTALLING THE PLUGIN:
	
1. Place the twitthis.php file inside your /wordpress/wp-content/plugins/ folder.

2. Activate it by going into your WordPress admin, clicking on the "Plugins" tab, and clicking on the "Activate" link next to the "TwitThis" plugin.

3. Change the options by going to Options > TwitThis.
